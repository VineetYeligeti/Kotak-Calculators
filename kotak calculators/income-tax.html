Claims on Tax deductions

Section 87A: Income below ₹ 5 lakh is eligible for a tax rebate of up to ₹ 12,500.

Section 80CCD (1B): For exemption of up to ₹ 2 lakh for money deposited in the  National Pension System.

Section 80C: Rebate of up to ₹ 1.5 lakh in any tax-saver fixed deposits, Public Provident Funds (PPF), National Savings Certificate, Unit-linked Insurance Plans, and Equity-linked Savings Schemes on the Interest Income.

Section 80D:  Up to ₹ 25,000 tax exemption on Medical Insurance Premium bills. The limit rises to ₹ 50,000 for senior citizens.

Section 80G: Any donations made to charitable organizations is fully exempt from tax calculations.

Section 80E: Interest on education loan enjoys a  100% tax rebate for up to 8 years.
